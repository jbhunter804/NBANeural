About

How to Use

Predictions are posted to the table above. In the "Market ML" columns are listed the payoffs according to betnow.eu. The "Model ML" columns list the payoffs that the model would consider to be fair. Therefore, the recommended strategy would be to bet on whatever team has a higher Market ML payoff than its Model ML payoff. Essentially, any payoff the market is offering in excess of the model's payoff is free upside. Collect as much of this upside as possible to maximize your expected return.

Where to Use

The model was trained on exclusively regular season MoneyLine wagers. While a good score from the model may be correlated with the game's spread, that is not the intended target for these predictions.

Timing

This site will automatically populate with predictions 15-30 minutes from the start of each game. Why so late? NBA teams need to finalize their rosters 30 minutes from tipoff. Therefore, in order to collect data on only players on the active roster, the model waits until the roster is locked in to analyze the lineups. This is particularly important in the COVID environment, as inactive lists have ballooned and become much less predictable.

Methodology

This model applies deep learning to NBA statistics. It was trained on data from eight NBA seasons with player-level data collected on basic statistics (points, assists, etc.), clutch statistics (performance in close games), momentum statistics (how have the last 10 and 20 games gone), and "advanced" statistics (according to NBA.com).

Notes
